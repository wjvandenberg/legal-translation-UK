## Notes

- **Industrial Property Code (1995. évi XXXIII. törvény)**: Governs patents, utility models,
  trademarks, and designs in Hungary. Reference as "Industrial Property Code" on first
  mention. Hungary participates in the European Patent Convention and the Unitary Patent
  system.
- **Copyright Act (1999. évi LXXVI. törvény)**: Governs copyright and neighbouring rights.
  Moral rights are non-transferable under Hungarian law. Economic rights are transferable.
  Flag the moral rights restriction.
- **Szellemi Tulajdon Nemzeti Hivatala (SZTNH)**: The Hungarian Intellectual Property
  Office. Handles patent, trademark, and design registrations. Reference as "SZTNH" or
  "Hungarian IP Office" on first mention.
- **"Szolgálati találmány" (employee invention)**: Under the Industrial Property Code,
  inventions created by employees in the course of employment belong to the employer, but
  the employee is entitled to remuneration. Flag when translating employment-related IP
  provisions.
- **EU Trade Secrets Directive**: Hungary implemented the Directive through the Trade
  Secrets Act (2018. évi LIV. törvény). Provides civil remedies for unlawful acquisition,
  use, and disclosure of trade secrets. Reference as "Trade Secrets Act."
- **UPC participation**: Hungary has ratified the Unified Patent Court Agreement. The UPC
  has jurisdiction over Unitary Patents and European patents in Hungary. Flag when
  translating patent litigation documents.

## Notes

- **GDPR and Hungarian data protection**: Hungary implements GDPR supplemented by the
  Freedom of Information Act (infotörvény, 2011. évi CXII. törvény). The NAIH is the
  supervisory authority. Cloud and SaaS contracts must address GDPR requirements, including
  data processing agreements and cross-border transfer mechanisms. Flag GDPR provisions.
- **Copyright Act — software**: Software is protected as a literary work under the Copyright
  Act (1999. évi LXXVI. törvény). The employer acquires economic rights to software
  created by employees in the course of employment (szolgálati mű). For commissioned
  software, rights must be contractually transferred. Flag the distinction.
- **E-Commerce Act (2001. évi CVIII. törvény)**: Governs information society services,
  including SaaS. Implements the EU E-Commerce Directive. Sets requirements for service
  provider identification, contracts concluded by electronic means, and liability of
  intermediary service providers. Reference as "E-Commerce Act."
- **Consumer protection in IT contracts**: The Consumer Protection Act and the Government
  Decree on distance contracts (45/2014. Korm. rendelet) apply to B2C SaaS and software
  agreements. 14-day withdrawal right for distance contracts. Flag when translating B2C
  IT agreements.
- **"Adatfeldolgozó" vs "adatkezelő"**: Hungarian data protection law follows the GDPR
  controller/processor distinction. Adatkezelő = data controller (determines purposes
  and means). Adatfeldolgozó = data processor (processes on behalf of controller). Ensure
  correct classification in DPA translations.
- **NIS2 implementation**: Hungary is implementing the EU NIS2 Directive for cybersecurity.
  Critical infrastructure operators and essential service providers face enhanced
  obligations. Flag cybersecurity requirements in IT contracts.
