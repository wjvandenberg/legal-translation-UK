# Polish — Corporate, M&A and JV Sub-Lexicon

Maps Polish corporate, M&A and joint-venture vocabulary to correct English equivalents.
Covers Kodeks spółek handlowych (k.s.h.) company forms, share deals, asset deals,
due diligence, JV/shareholders' agreements, and transactional documentation.

## Notes

- **k.s.h. (Kodeks spółek handlowych)**: The Code of Commercial Companies of 15 September
  2000 governs all Polish company forms except the civil-law partnership (s.c.) which is
  governed by the Civil Code. Always cite the k.s.h. when referring to company-law
  provisions.
- **Prosta spółka akcyjna (P.S.A.)** introduced 1 July 2021 — a modern company form designed
  for start-ups, combining features of the sp. z o.o. and the S.A. Features include minimum
  capital of PLN 1, share capital expressed without nominal value, and an option for a
  single-tier board (rada dyrektorów). Flag when encountered — the P.S.A. has no direct
  equivalent in most jurisdictions.
- **"Aport" (contribution in kind)**: Non-cash contributions are strictly regulated. For
  S.A., a biegły rewident (statutory auditor) must review the valuation. For sp. z o.o.,
  founders bear statutory liability for overvaluation of in-kind contributions (art. 175
  k.s.h.).
- **Bearer shares abolished (2021)**: Since the 2021 reform, all shares in non-listed
  Polish joint-stock companies are registered and dematerialised (rejestr akcjonariuszy).
  Translate "akcja na okaziciela" only when referring to historical instruments.
- **KRS (Krajowy Rejestr Sądowy)**: The National Court Register is Poland's commercial
  register, maintained by regional courts. Registrations are constitutive for entry formation
  (e.g., merger becomes effective upon KRS registration). Reference as "KRS" on first mention.
- **Closing mechanics**: Polish M&A practice uses both locked box and completion accounts
  mechanisms. The preferred approach depends on sector and timing. Share transfers in sp.
  z o.o. require written form with notarised signatures (art. 180 k.s.h.); S.A. share
  transfers typically require entry into the rejestr akcjonariuszy.
- **Notarised form requirement**: Certain corporate actions require notarial deed (forma
  aktu notarialnego) — e.g., formation of sp. z o.o. and S.A., amendments to articles of
  association, transformations. Flag when translating, as the requirement is mandatory.
- **UOKiK merger control**: Notification thresholds under the Act on Competition and
  Consumer Protection — combined turnover of the parties' groups exceeds EUR 1 billion
  worldwide or EUR 50 million in Poland. Standstill obligation applies pending clearance.
- **FDI screening**: Since 2020, acquisitions of "protected entities" in strategic sectors
  (energy, telecoms, defence, etc.) by investors from outside the EU/EEA/OECD require prior
  notification to the Prezes UOKiK. Flag in cross-border transactions.
- **Zgromadzenie wspólników vs walne zgromadzenie**: The shareholders' meeting of an sp.
  z o.o. is called "zgromadzenie wspólników"; the equivalent for S.A. is "walne
  zgromadzenie akcjonariuszy (WZA)". Translate as "shareholders' meeting" or "general
  meeting" as appropriate, noting the entity type.
