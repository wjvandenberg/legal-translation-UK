# Hungarian — NDAs and Service Agreements Sub-Lexicon

Maps Hungarian confidentiality and services vocabulary to correct English equivalents.
Covers titoktartási / bizalmassági megállapodások, and megbízási, vállalkozási,
keretszerződések, SLA-k, outsourcing, tanácsadási és managed services szerződések.

## Notes

- **Trade Secrets Act (2018. évi LIV. törvény)**: Hungary's trade secret legislation,
  implementing EU Directive 2016/943. Provides civil remedies for unlawful
  acquisition, use, and disclosure. Reference as "Trade Secrets Act" on first mention.
- **"Kötbér" (contractual penalty)**: Hungarian NDAs and services contracts commonly
  include kötbér. Under the Ptk. (6:186–6:189), kötbér is payable without proof of
  damage. Courts may reduce excessive penalties. In English translation render as
  "liquidated damages" in commercial context (avoid "penalty" as unenforceable under
  English law); use "service credits" in SLAs. Note the no-proof-of-damage feature.
- **Competition Act trade secret protection**: The Competition Act (1996. évi LVII.
  törvény) also provides trade secret protection through unfair-competition
  provisions. Dual remedies may be available under both the Trade Secrets Act and the
  Competition Act.
- **"Üzleti titok" (trade secret) definition**: Under the Trade Secrets Act, must be:
  (1) secret, (2) commercially valuable because of its secrecy, and (3) subject to
  reasonable steps to maintain secrecy. Aligns with the EU Directive definition.
- **GDPR implementation**: Hungary implemented GDPR supplemented by the Freedom of
  Information Act (infotörvény, 2011. évi CXII. törvény). The National Authority for
  Data Protection and Freedom of Information (NAIH) is the supervisory authority.
- **"Megbízás" vs "vállalkozás"**: Fundamental Hungarian-law distinction. Megbízási
  szerződés (Ptk. 6:272–6:280) = mandate — best-efforts obligation, similar to a
  service agreement. Vállalkozási szerződés (Ptk. 6:238–6:271) = contract for work —
  results-based obligation. Under megbízás, the provider exercises due care; under
  vállalkozás, the provider guarantees a result. The classification drives warranty,
  acceptance and liability rules — always identify the applicable type.
- **"Kellékszavatosság" (warranty for defects)**: Under vállalkozási szerződés, the
  contractor is liable for defects (Ptk. 6:159–6:167). Remedies include repair,
  replacement, price reduction and rescission. Limitation period is 1 year from
  acceptance.
- **VAT**: Hungary's standard VAT rate is 27% — the highest in the EU. Reduced rates
  apply to specific categories. Reverse charge applies to certain domestic and
  cross-border services.
- **"Szolgálati mű" (work made for hire)**: Under the Copyright Act, the employer
  acquires economic rights to works created by employees in the course of employment.
  For commissioned works, the rights must be contractually transferred. Flag the
  distinction.
