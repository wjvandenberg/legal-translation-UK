## Grammar Notes (German-Specific)

- **Compound nouns**: German creates single compound words where English uses phrases
  ("Gesellschafterversammlungsbeschluss" → "shareholders' meeting resolution")
- **Noun capitalisation**: All German nouns are capitalised; English only capitalises
  defined terms and proper nouns
- **"Soweit"**: Context-dependent — "to the extent that" or "insofar as" or "provided that"
- **"Unbeschadet"**: "Without prejudice to" or "notwithstanding"
- **"Vorbehaltlich"**: "Subject to"
- **"Gegebenenfalls" (ggf.)**: "As the case may be" or "where applicable"
- **"Im Sinne von"**: "Within the meaning of"
- **"Gemäß"**: "Pursuant to" or "in accordance with"
