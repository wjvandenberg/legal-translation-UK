# Hungarian — Transport and Insurance Sub-Lexicon

Combines Hungarian-language transport and insurance vocabulary. Part 1 covers maritime, road, rail, air and multimodal transport: carriage contracts, bills of lading, charterparties, freight forwarding, carrier liability. Part 2 covers insurance contracts, life and non-life lines, reinsurance, brokers, claims handling and regulatory framework. Marine/transport insurance (cargo, hull, P&I, carrier liability) sits at the intersection.

## Notes

- **Water Transport Act (2000. évi XLII. törvény)**: Hungary's primary maritime and inland
  waterway legislation. Covers vessel registration, carrier liability, and navigation on
  the Danube. Reference as "Water Transport Act" on first mention.
- **Danube navigation**: Hungary is a landlocked country but has significant inland
  waterway transport on the Danube. The Budapest Convention on the Contract for the
  Carriage of Goods by Inland Waterway (CMNI) applies. Flag for Danube-specific provisions.
- **CMR Convention**: Hungary is a party to the CMR Convention for international road
  carriage of goods. The CMR consignment note is the standard transport document. Carrier
  liability is strict with limited defences (UK) / defenses (US). Flag CMR applicability.
- **Incoterms**: Widely used in Hungarian international trade. The Hungarian Chamber of
  Commerce provides official translations. Use the standard Incoterms abbreviations with
  the year (e.g., "FOB (Incoterms 2020)").
- **"Közös hajókár" (general average)**: Applies under the York-Antwerp Rules when
  referenced in the charterparty. Translate as "general average" and reference the
  applicable York-Antwerp Rules version.
- **Customs Union / EU**: Hungary is part of the EU Customs Union. The Union Customs Code
  (UCC) applies to all customs procedures. Flag EU customs provisions.

## Notes

- **Insurance Act (2014. évi LXXXVIII. törvény)**: The primary legislation governing
  insurance contracts in Hungary. Replaced the earlier insurance provisions in the Civil
  Code. Reference as "Insurance Act" on first mention.
- **Solvency II**: Hungary implements the EU Solvency II framework through the Insurance
  Act and MNB (Magyar Nemzeti Bank) regulations. The MNB is the insurance supervisory
  authority. Flag Solvency II requirements.
- **MNB as insurance supervisor**: The Magyar Nemzeti Bank (Hungarian National Bank) acts
  as the supervisory authority for insurance companies, in addition to its central banking
  role. Reference as "MNB" after first mention.
- **"Kötelező gépjármű-felelősségbiztosítás" (compulsory MTPL)**: Hungarian law requires
  motor third-party liability insurance. Governed by a separate statute (2009. évi LXII.
  törvény). The MABISZ (Magyar Biztosítók Szövetsége / Association of Hungarian Insurers)
  operates the compensation fund. Flag when translating motor insurance documents.
- **"Önrész" (deductible/excess)**: Hungarian insurance contracts commonly use both fixed
  and percentage-based deductibles. Translate as "deductible" for US-oriented texts and
  "excess" for UK-oriented texts.
- **Ptk. insurance provisions**: The Civil Code (Ptk.) retains general rules applicable
  to insurance contracts (Book 6, Title XXII). The Insurance Act is lex specialis. Flag
  when Ptk. provisions apply.
