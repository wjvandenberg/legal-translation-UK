## Notes

- **"Cláusula abusiva"**: Under Spanish law (TRLGDCU), unfair terms in consumer contracts
  are void. Based on the EU Unfair Contract Terms Directive. Translate as "unfair term".
- **"Indemnización por clientela"**: Under Spanish agency law (transposing the EU Commercial
  Agents Directive), the commercial agent is entitled to a goodwill indemnity on termination.
  Mandatory and cannot be waived in advance.
- **"Derecho de desistimiento"**: The consumer's right to withdraw from a distance or
  off-premises contract within 14 days. Based on the EU Consumer Rights Directive.
- **TRLGDCU**: Texto Refundido de la Ley General para la Defensa de los Consumidores y
  Usuarios — the consolidated Spanish consumer protection act. Reference as "Spanish
  Consumer Protection Act" on first mention.
