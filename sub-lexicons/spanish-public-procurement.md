## Notes

- **Ley de Contratos del Sector Público (LCSP)**: The Spanish Public Procurement Act
  (Ley 9/2017). Reference as "Spanish Public Procurement Act" on first mention.
- **"Unión temporal de empresas" (UTE)**: A specifically Spanish legal form of consortium
  for public procurement. Members are jointly and severally liable. Translate as
  "consortium" or "temporary joint venture" with context note.
- **"Resolución por interés público"**: The contracting authority's prerogative to terminate
  for public interest reasons. Similar to termination for convenience but with specific
  compensation rules under Spanish law.
- **"Riesgo y ventura"**: The Spanish principle that the contractor bears the economic risk.
  See energy-infrastructure sub-lexicon for more detail.
