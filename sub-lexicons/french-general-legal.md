## Grammar Notes (French-Specific)

- **Adjective placement**: French often places adjectives after nouns;
  English places them before ("obligations contractuelles existantes" → "existing contractual obligations")
- **"Étant entendu que"**: "it being understood that" or "provided that"
- **"Sous réserve de"**: "subject to" or "without prejudice to"
- **"Le cas échéant"**: "as the case may be" or "where applicable"
- **"Nonobstant"**: "notwithstanding"
- **"Sans préjudice de"**: "without prejudice to"
- **"Tout(e)… quel(le) qu'il/elle soit"**: "any … whatsoever"
- **"À défaut"**: "failing which" or "in the absence of"
