## Notes

- **Land register (ingatlan-nyilvántartás)**: Hungary operates a title registration system
  administered by the district land registry offices (járási földhivatal). All rights and
  encumbrances must be registered to be effective against third parties. The land register
  has public faith (közhitelesség). Flag registration requirements.
- **"Foglaló" (earnest money)**: Under the Ptk. (6:185), the foglaló serves as both
  confirmation of the contract and a security. If the buyer defaults, the foglaló is
  forfeited. If the seller defaults, the buyer can claim double the foglaló. Translate as
  "earnest money" and explain the doubled-return feature.
- **Agricultural land restrictions**: Acquisition of agricultural land is subject to strict
  restrictions under the Agricultural Land Act (2013. évi CXXII. törvény). Local land
  committees must approve transfers. Non-EU citizens generally cannot acquire agricultural
  land. Flag when translating agricultural land transactions.
- **Foreign ownership**: No general restriction on foreign ownership of real estate (except
  agricultural land). EU/EEA nationals and legal persons are treated equally. Non-EU
  persons need a permit from the local government office. Flag foreign buyer provisions.
- **"Ügyvédi ellenjegyzés" (attorney countersignature)**: Real estate transfer contracts
  must be countersigned by a Hungarian attorney (ügyvéd) to be eligible for registration
  in the land register. This is a mandatory formal requirement. Translate as "attorney
  countersignature" and note the requirement.
- **Transfer tax (illeték)**: Real estate transfers are subject to a 4% transfer tax
  (visszterhes vagyonátruházási illeték) on the market value. Exemptions and reductions
  apply in specific cases. Flag the tax obligation.
