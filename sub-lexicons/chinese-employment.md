## Notes

- **PRC Labour Contract Law (劳动合同法)**: Enacted 2007, amended 2012. The principal
  statute governing individual employment relationships. Reference as "Labour Contract
  Law" on first mention.
- **"经济补偿金" (statutory severance — "N")**: Calculated as one month's salary per year
  of service (Art. 47 Labour Contract Law). Monthly salary is capped at three times the
  local average salary; total years capped at 12 for high earners. The "+1" in "N+1"
  is one month's payment in lieu of 30-day notice. "2N" applies to unlawful dismissal.
- **"劳动仲裁前置"**: Labour disputes must first go through arbitration (labour dispute
  arbitration committee) before litigation. This is mandatory — courts will not accept
  cases without a prior arbitration award or certificate. Translate as "mandatory labour
  arbitration" and flag the procedural requirement.
- **"竞业限制" (Arts. 23-24 Labour Contract Law)**: Post-employment non-competes are
  limited to senior management, senior technicians, and persons with confidentiality
  obligations. Maximum duration: 2 years. Monthly compensation must be paid (typically
  30% of pre-termination salary). Without compensation for 3+ months, the employee may
  terminate the non-compete.
- **"五险一金" (five insurances + one fund)**: The mandatory social insurance package
  (pension, medical, unemployment, work injury, maternity) plus the housing provident
  fund. Contributions are shared between employer and employee at rates set by local
  governments. Flag when translating compensation provisions.
- **"事实劳动关系"**: If an employer fails to execute a written contract within one month
  of commencement, a de facto employment relationship is deemed to exist, and the employee
  is entitled to double wages for up to 11 months. After one year, an open-ended contract
  is deemed concluded. Flag in translation.
