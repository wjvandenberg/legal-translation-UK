# Hungarian — Permitting and Environmental Law Sub-Lexicon

Maps Hungarian environmental and permitting vocabulary to correct English
equivalents. Covers EIA, integrated environmental use permits, water, waste,
nature, chemicals, contaminated land, climate, and construction/zoning.

## Notes

- **Kvt. (Act LIII of 1995)**: General framework act on environmental
  protection. Sets out principles (precautionary, preventive, polluter-pays),
  main instruments (permits, EIA, environmental damage), and institutional
  framework. Amended frequently — verify current consolidation.
- **Integrated permitting (EKHE)**: Government Decree 314/2005 operationalises
  both EIA and IED integrated-permit procedures. EKHE (egységes
  környezethasználati engedély) is the Hungarian IPPC/IED integrated permit
  — required for Annex 2 activities. Combined procedures where EIA and EKHE
  run concurrently. Competent authority: county government office (kormány-
  hivatal) with specialised departments.
- **Éghajlatvédelmi törvény (2020)**: Hungary's Climate Protection Act sets
  climate-neutrality target for 2050 and declares climate emergency. Updated
  by the Climate Neutrality Act of 2024. Binding emissions-reduction targets,
  long-term climate strategy, and coal phase-out by 2030 (except Mátra).
  Critical in energy-project translations.
- **Government office reorganisation**: Environmental-permit competence
  consolidated from former separate Környezetvédelmi Felügyelőségek into
  the county government offices (kormányhivatal) environmental departments.
  Since 2015 reforms, inspection and enforcement also with government offices.
  Translate "kormányhivatal" as "government office" consistently; may specify
  "county" or particular county name.
- **Natura 2000 implementation**: Hungarian Natura 2000 sites designated by
  275/2004. (X. 8.) Korm. rendelet. Hungary has large protected-area
  network. Nature-protection authority consent required for activities
  affecting Natura 2000 sites. Appropriate assessment integrated with EIA.
- **Hulladéktörvény and MOHU**: Since July 2023, waste management in Hungary
  is operated through a single concessionaire — MOHU (MOL Hulladékgazdálkodási
  Zrt.) — under state concession. Major shift from prior municipal/private
  model. Producer responsibility (EPR) scheme restructured in parallel.
  Flag in all post-2023 waste translations.
- **Soil contamination — B/C/K values**: Decree 6/2009 (KvVM-EüM-FVM)
  specifies substance-specific contamination limit values. "B" (background)
  for pristine soils, "C" (contamination) triggering remediation planning,
  "K" (kármentesítési) remediation target values. Real-estate due diligence
  standard.
- **Administrative litigation reform**: 2018 reform — Act I of 2017 on
  Administrative Procedure (Ákr.) and Act I of 2017 on Administrative Court
  Procedure (Kp.). Environmental appeals go to government office
  (first instance) → Budapest Metropolitan Court → Curia. No separate
  environmental court. Standing of environmental NGOs recognised under
  Aarhus.
- **Seveso — Decree 219/2011**: Implements Seveso III. Lower-tier requires
  biztonsági elemzés (safety analysis); upper-tier requires biztonsági
  jelentés (safety report). External emergency plans developed by Disaster
  Management Directorate (OKF). Relevant for chemical-industrial facilities.
- **VGT and VKI implementation**: Hungary's River Basin Management Plans (VGT)
  covering all water bodies in Hungarian territory (parts of Danube,
  Tisza, Balaton). Prepared by OVF; adopted by Government Decree. WFD-Hungary
  compliance has been subject to infringement proceedings.
- **Construction permitting transition**: Hungary has moved between municipal
  and state-centralised building permits multiple times. Current regime
  (2024): single-family homes via "egyszerű bejelentés" (simple notification)
  to municipal notary; larger projects via "építési engedély". Special
  regimes for priority investments (kiemelt beruházás). Verify applicable
  regime at translation date.
- **Cross-references**: See `hungarian-real-estate.md` for building permits
  and zoning; `hungarian-energy-infrastructure.md` for renewables permitting
  and ETS; `hungarian-corporate-ma-jv.md` for environmental reps and
  warranties; `hungarian-litigation-settlement.md` for administrative-court
  procedure.
