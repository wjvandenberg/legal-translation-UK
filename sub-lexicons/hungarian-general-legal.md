## Notes

- **Hungarian Civil Code (Ptk.)**: The Civil Code of 2013 (Act V of 2013), effective March
  2014, is Hungary's comprehensive civil law codification covering persons, property, family,
  obligations (including contracts), and succession. All contract law references should cite
  the Ptk. rather than the superseded 1959 Civil Code.
- **Hungarian numbering convention**: Hungarian legislation uses § (section), followed by
  bekezdés (subsection) and pont (item). A reference like "6:62. § (2) bekezdés" means
  Book 6, Section 62, Subsection (2). Preserve the structure.
- **EU member state**: Hungary is an EU member. EU regulations apply directly. EU directives
  are implemented through national legislation. Always check for EU law dimensions.
- **"Jóhiszeműség és tisztesség"**: The principle of good faith and fair dealing is a
  fundamental principle in Hungarian civil law (Ptk. 1:3. §). Courts apply it broadly.
  Translate as "good faith and fair dealing".
- **"Elévülés" (limitation)**: The general limitation period under the Ptk. is 5 years.
  Special limitation periods apply to specific claims. The Ptk. allows for contractual
  modification of limitation periods within certain limits. Flag when translating.
