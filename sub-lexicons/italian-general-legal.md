## Grammar Notes (Italian-Specific)

- **Adjective placement**: Italian places adjectives after nouns; English before
  ("impianti esistenti e futuri" → "existing and future plants")
- **Double negatives**: Italian legal drafting uses double negatives frequently
  ("non può non" → "must" or "shall necessarily")
- **Gerund constructions**: "avendo le parti convenuto" → "the Parties having agreed"
  or "since the Parties have agreed"
- **"Del buon padre di famiglia"**: Standard of care → "reasonable care"
